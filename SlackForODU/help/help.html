  • About

  • What is SlackForODU?

    SlackForODU is an online platform for students of the Old Dominion University to socialize and share their thoughts. The portal helps students, faculty, staff and the like to connect with new people and communicate shared interests.

  • Who is it for?

    SlackForODU is primarily for faculty, staff and students of ODU.

  • Get Started

  • How do I get started?

    Create or join a workspace.
    Create or join a channel.
    Change your profile.
    Start posting!

  • How to get access to a workspace?

    Get to know the URL of the workspace you want to be a part of. Ask the administrator or other users of the workspace.

  • Account

  • How do I create my account?

    Enter into a workspace you want to be a part of, by entering the URL.
    Click "Sign Up".
    Enter your credentials.
    Upload a profile picture (optional).
    Click "Next".
    Your profile is created!
    Login with your credentials to start posting.

  • How do I login to my account?

    Enter the URL of the workspace you are a part of.
    Enter your credentials (username & password).
    Click "Sign in".

  • How do I edit my profile picture?

    Click "See your profile" on sidebar.
    Click on the default or existing profile picture.
    Click "Choose File" to upload new picture.
    Click "Next".

  • Maximum image size for profile picture.

    Your profile picture can be of maximum 10 MB size.

  • I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

    Ask the administrator of your workspace to reset your password.

  • Messages

  • Posting to a channel.

    Users can post messages in existing channels or create new channels and start posting.

    Click on a channel name from a list of channels on the sidebar.
    Type your message in the textbox (located at the bottom of chat area).
    Hit enter or click "Send" to make a post in the channel.

  • Initiate a direct message.

    Users can initiate private conversations with other users of the workspace.

    Click on a username from the list of users on the sidebar.
    Type your message in the textbox (located at the bottom of chat area).
    Hit enter or click "Send" to make a direct post.

  • How to reply to a post?

    Users can make a reply to a post, initiating or adding to a thread of message replies.
    Every post has its individual textbox to make a reply.
    Enter text in the textbox.
    Hit enter or click "Send" to make a reply.

  • What are reactions?

    A user can "like" or "dislike" a post expressing their reaction on how they feel about it.

    Click the button to like a post.
    Click the button to dislike a post.

  • How can I post images/pictures?

    Click the icon next to the textbox at the bottom of chat area.
    Click on "Choose File" to upload an image file from local computer.
    OR
    Enter the URL of an image to upload image from the Web.
    Click "Next".

  • How can I post pretty formatted text?

    Before entering any text you want to pretty format and send as a post, append ">" before the text.

  • Channels

  • What are channels?

    Every workspace has designated channels that members can join to share common interests. The purpose of having channels is to enable users with common interests to come together and have discussions about them.
    Example: #fashion, #travel

  • How do I join a channel?

    Ask the administrator or other users of the workspace to invite you to join a channel.

  • How to create a new channel?

    Users of a workspace can create new channels and invite other users to the channel.

    Click on the button next to Channels on the sidebar.
    Enter details about your new channel (name, purpose).
    Invite users to your channel by clicking on their usernames from the selection box.
    Make your channel "Public" or "Private".
    Click "Create channel".

  • What are private channels?

    Users who are not members of a channel, do not have access to posts of a private channel. Private channels can be joined by invitation only.

  • What are archived channels?

    The administrator can archive a channel. Users cannot post into archived channels, but can only view posts prior to channel's archival.

  • How to invite users to join a channel?

    Click "Details" next to the channel name on the sidebar.
    Enter the username of people you want to invite to your channel.
    Click "Invite".

  • How are invited users notified?

    The icon on top corner of sidebar turns red when a user has channel invitations.

  • How to accept or decline channel invitations?

    The icon on top corner of sidebar turns red when a user has channel invitations.
    Click on the "Bell Notification" icon.
    Click "Accept" to accept a channel invitation.
    OR
    Click "Decline" to decline a channel invitation.

  • What are #general and #random channels?

    Every workspace has a #general and a #random channels by default.
    All the users of a workspace are members of these channels.
    Make common posts in #general channel.
    Make casual posts in #random channel.

  • Channel names.

    Channel names must be lowercase, without spaces or periods, and shorter than 22 characters.

  • Other

  • How to look for other people's profiles?

    A user can search for other users in the workspace.

    Click "Search other's profile" on the sidebar.
    Enter their name in the search bar. Name suggestions appear as you type.
    Click "Submit".
